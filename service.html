Our services

Happy Paws is engaged towards the welfare of all animals. We provide services from pet health to pet sitting.
We not only provide shelter to the animals but we also ensure that they get the best family.
That is why we firmly encourage adoption and dream about a world where animals live peacefully.

Pet Health Animal Care

If you love your pets, you would do anything to give them the most excellent health possible.
At Happy Paws we offer you free veterinary advice as well as the best offers for your pet's health.
It is important to ensure that your pet is well vaccinated against all potential diseasess.

Animals need medical care but as well as lots of love and affection. It is important to make sure that your pet is not ill-treated. Just like humans, animals need intensive loving and caring.
